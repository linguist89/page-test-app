About Ducks

Ducks are fascinating waterfowl that can be found all around the world. They are known for their waterproof feathers, distinctive quacking sounds, and their ability to swim gracefully on water.

Interesting Duck Facts

Adaptable Creatures

Ducks can live in both freshwater and saltwater environments, and some species migrate thousands of miles each year.

Swimming Experts

A duck's feathers are naturally waterproof, thanks to an oil gland that helps them stay dry even while swimming.

Diet Diversity

Ducks are omnivorous and eat a variety of foods including plants, insects, small fish, and aquatic creatures.
